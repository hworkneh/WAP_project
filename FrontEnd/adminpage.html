Welcome to Golden Dome Market

Golden Dome Market and Café is one of the markets which specialized in offering organic food located north of the Ladies’ Golden Dome in Fairfield Iowa. As we all are social distancing due to the current COVID-2019 situation, all Maharishi International University students are grocery shopping through Golden Dome Market. Over the past few days, we have been using excel sheet provided by the university to receive our order which allows us to enter items and brand. While this has been a great opportunity to fill our need, we see huge gap between the students need and the service provided by the market.We are intending to implement Dynamic Web Application where the students can fill their order, get confirmation of receiving their order, and will eventually receive notification when the ordered items are ready to pick.
